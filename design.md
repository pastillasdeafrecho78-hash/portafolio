### Scale
- H1: `clamp(2rem, 5vw, 3.5rem)` — line-height 1.1
- H2: `clamp(1.5rem, 3vw, 2.25rem)` — line-height 1.2
- Body: `1rem` / `1.125rem` — line-height 1.6
- Small: `0.875rem` — line-height 1.5

## Spacing & Radius
- Section padding: `py-24 md:py-32`
- Container max-width: `1280px`
- Card radius: `12px`
- Button radius: `8px`
- Pill radius: `9999px`
- Gap grid: `24px` / `32px`

## Motion
- Duration: `200ms`–`400ms`
- Easing: `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`
- Only animate: `opacity`, `transform`
- Video: GSAP timelines, seek-driven
